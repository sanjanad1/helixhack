COVID-19


COVID-19 is a newly discovered type of coronavirus. Scientists have recently found out that this coronavirus doesn't cause harmless and mild symptoms like the other coronaviruses. It can be a deathly disease to people with weak immune systems.

How Does it Spread?


COVID-19 is a virus that has affected many people. It is easily can spread to many people from a small cough or sneeze. COVID-19 can be spread from a 6-foot distance between two people.

Preventing COVID-19 Cases


Recently, the whole world has been affected by COVID-19 including over 19.4 million cases and over 700,000 deaths. This virus is easily spread and everyone should do what they can to prevent such a contagious disease. You can practice basic things like a 6-foot distance when outside, quarantine at home, and wear a mask when near others.
